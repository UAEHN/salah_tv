Privacy Policy for Mawaqit TV

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Introduction

Mawaqit TV is an Islamic prayer times display application for Android TV devices. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. We do not collect names, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

Internet Usage

The App requires internet access solely to stream Quran audio from mp3quran.net.

Local Storage

The App stores your selected city and prayer settings locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Prayer Times Data

Prayer times are bundled within the App as a local database. No external prayer time API calls are made.

Third-Party Services

The App streams Quran audio from mp3quran.net. Please refer to their privacy policy for how they handle streaming requests.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from users of any age.
